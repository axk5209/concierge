COVID-19

Keeping your employees safe

COVID-19 is highly contagious, and more variants are rising. Due to continued COVID-19 case growth in Texas and very recent CDC data showing increased transmissibility of the delta variant (including some cases of viral carriage in asymptomatic, fully vaccinated individuals), testing is strongly recommended to halt the spread and put an end to this pandemic.
FirstMD VIP provides a turnkey solution for keeping workspace safe, allowing business to run smoothly, and providing access to covid related care at workspace.
We document the test results into a secure HIPAA compliant health portal. This ensures ease of use for the user and accurately tracks all employees COVID status. We leverage the best of technologies to provide a world-class care experience.
