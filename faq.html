Frequently Asked Questions

Do employees pay for the COVID-19 tests?

No, neither the employee nor the employer pays have to pay for any COVID-19 related tests.

How do I sign up? Can I sign up as an employee?

Your employer will sign up with us and you can then avail our services.

Is my data secure?

All our software are HIPAA compliant.

How can I see my COVID-19 test results? Will the results be shared with my employer?

The test results will not be shared with your employer unless authorized by you.

What is the difference between rapid test and RT-PCR test?

Rapid test is antigen test done at the office and results are available in 15 minutes. Rt PCR is current gold standard diagnostic and usually done at a Lab which takes over 24 hrs to result.

How long can I access my data?

You can sign up for patient portal which will allow access to your data as long as your remain a patient with the practice

What is mobile service? Do I pay extra for this service?

Mobile service is included and is part of our concierge service with a promise to deliver health care at your door steps

Will my insurance premium go up?

Our goal is make you choose a healthy lifestyle which will have positive impact in your health and reduce overall healthcare cost. This should in fact help lower your premium.
