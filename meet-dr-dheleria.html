Meet Dr. Dhelaria

Ranjit Dhelaria, MD, MRCP(UK)



Dr. Dhelaria is a member of Royal College of Physicians of United Kingdom and has received excellent training in Internal Medicine at the western campus of Tufts University School of Medicine. He has completed his Nephrology and Hypertension fellowship at the renowned Boston University and is dual board certified. He is also a fellow of the American Society of Nephrology. He has co-authored several publications in peer-reviewed journals and has participated in multiple cutting-edge research trials during his career. He has held several leadership positions during his professional life and served on multiple nonprofit organizations in various roles.

Born and raised in India, Dr. Dhelaria has a very unique journey through his professional life. He has travelled the world and experienced a wide variety of medical culture and practices across different countries which has broadened his experience. Given his extensive training, he has an intricate understanding of chronic medical conditions, which allows him to design and tailor medical care to individual needs.
