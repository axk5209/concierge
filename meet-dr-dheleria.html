Meet Dr. Dhelaria


Ranjit Dhelaria, MD



Dr Ranjit Dhelaria, MD, MRCP (UK) is an associate professor of Medicine at UNT health Sciences. Dr. Dhelaria received his post graduate training in Internal Medicine at the western campus of Tufts University. He is dual board certified and is a Member of Royal college of Physicians of United Kingdom. Dr. Dhelaria is an established and highly skilled physician with over 15 years of experience and is well regarded in the medical community.

Dr Dhelaria has a very simple philosophy and hence with his unique program which is specifically designed to help small businesses to large corporation to provide access to concierge- level service at a fraction of the cost of health insurance. Wellness at work is designed to address the overall wellbeing of an individual and taking into special consideration their emotional health and nutrition.
