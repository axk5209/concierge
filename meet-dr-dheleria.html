Meet Dr. Dheleria

Ranjit Dhelaria, MD, MRCP(UK)

Dr. Dheleria is an Associate Professor of Medicine at UNT Health Sciences and Fellow of American Society of Nephrology. He is dual board certified and is a diplomate of American Board of Internal Medicine. He has been practicing Nephrology for several years. He is committed to finding and treating kidney disease early in its presentation and is passionate about advocating for his patients. Because the kidney performs so many critical functions, Dr Dhelaria not only maintains expertise in primary kidney disorders, but also the management of the systemic consequences of kidney dysfunction.
