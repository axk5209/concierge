What is Concierge Primary Care?



FirstMD VIP was created with one simple concept in mind - offering the same level of care to all patients as I do my friends and family. You can now enjoy a direct, personal relationship with your primary care doctor. We put you back in control of your health care experience.
We have open and flexible office hours, paired with latest communication tools and unmatched access to your physician - through an in-person appointment or through text, phone, email, or televisit.
Same day, after-hours, and weekend appointments are available in most cases. Relaxed, 30-60 minute appointments will allow you all the time you need with your doctor.
We make house calls at the discretion of the physicians and need for the patient. We can provide care at the privacy of your own home. With this service, you can enjoy concierge medical service at your own comfort.
If you are looking for a primary care physician in Roanoke, Mansfield or the surrounding areas, request a risk-free consultation today.
