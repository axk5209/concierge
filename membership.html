Benefits of Membership

For members of the Concierge Medicine program, there are no barriers between you and your family doctor. Members will receive an executive physical along with direct access to our physician 24/7 via text, email, and/or phone call.
As a member you can see the doctor as often as you need and have enhanced appointment availability, usually the same day. We optimize your health through individualized care that is focused on preventive and proactive medicine. We provide office-based procedures at each of these locations. We also have discount pricing on laboratory work for patients without medical insurance.
Our promise is simple - to make our patients feel right at home while we partner with you and your health goals. If you are ready to get your life and your health back, there is not better place to start than FirstMD VIP Concierge Medicine.
